About us

Welcome to Amrita College, Bengaluru Campus

Nestled in the bustling tech hub of India's Silicon Valley, Amrita College's Bengaluru campus stands as a beacon of excellence in education, research, and innovation. Our campus, spread across a sprawling green expanse, offers a serene and conducive environment for academic pursuits and holistic development.

Our Vision

At Amrita College, we are dedicated to fostering a learning environment that nurtures the intellectual, ethical, and professional growth of our students. Our vision is to shape future leaders who are not only academically proficient but also socially responsible and globally aware.

Academic Excellence

Our Bengaluru campus is renowned for its rigorous academic programs, cutting-edge research facilities, and distinguished faculty. We offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, humanities, and sciences. Our curriculum is designed to be industry-relevant, incorporating the latest advancements and trends to ensure our students are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern world.

Research and Innovation

Innovation is at the heart of everything we do at Amrita College. Our state-of-the-art research centers and laboratories provide students and faculty with the resources to explore new ideas and create groundbreaking solutions. We encourage a culture of curiosity and creativity, fostering an environment where innovative thinking and collaboration thrive.

Campus Life

Life at Amrita College, Bengaluru, is vibrant and enriching. Our campus is equipped with world-class amenities, including modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, a comprehensive library, sports facilities, and comfortable residential accommodations. We believe in the holistic development of our students, and our campus life is designed to nurture their physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Community Engagement

We are committed to making a positive impact on society. Our students and faculty actively engage in community service and outreach programs, contributing to the betterment of the local community and beyond. Through various initiatives, we aim to instill a sense of social responsibility and encourage our students to be proactive in addressing societal challenges.

Global Opportunities

In today's interconnected world, global exposure is crucial for personal and professional growth. At Amrita College, Bengaluru, we offer numerous opportunities for international collaboration, student exchange programs, and global internships. Our partnerships with leading universities and organizations worldwide provide our students with a global perspective and enhance their learning experience.

Alumni Network

Our alumni are our pride and strength. The Amrita College alumni network is a thriving community of professionals who are making significant contributions to various fields across the globe. We maintain strong ties with our alumni, fostering a sense of belonging and collaboration that continues well beyond graduation.

Join Us

We invite you to be a part of the Amrita College family. Whether you are a prospective student, a researcher, or a collaborator, we welcome you to explore the myriad opportunities that our Bengaluru campus has to offer. Together, let's embark on a journey of learning, discovery, and growth.
