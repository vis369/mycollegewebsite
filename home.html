Welcome to Amrita College, Bengaluru Campus

Discover Excellence in Education

At Amrita College, Bengaluru, we believe in nurturing the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and innovators.
Our campus, located in the heart of India’s tech capital, offers a perfect blend of academic rigor, research opportunities,
and vibrant campus life. Explore our diverse programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a community committed to your success.

Why Choose Amrita College?

1. Academic Excellence:
Our globally recognized faculty, cutting-edge curriculum, and world-class infrastructure ensure a learning experience that is both comprehensive and contemporary.
We offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs tailored to equip you with the knowledge and skills required in today’s dynamic world.
2. Research and Innovation:
At Amrita, we foster a culture of innovation and research.
Our dedicated research centers and partnerships with leading global institutions provide students with unparalleled opportunities to engage in groundbreaking projects and real-world problem solving.
3. Holistic Development:
We believe in the holistic development of our students.
Our campus offers a plethora of extracurricular activities, sports, and wellness programs designed to nurture your physical, mental, and emotional well-being.
4. Global Opportunities:
Amrita College, Bengaluru, is your gateway to global exposure.
Through our extensive network of international collaborations, student exchange programs, and internships, we prepare you to thrive in a globalized world.
5. Community Engagement:
We are committed to making a positive impact on society.
Our students actively participate in community service and outreach programs, embodying the values of empathy, responsibility, and social contribution.
